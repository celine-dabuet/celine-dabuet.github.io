Te Whare Tapa Whā

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

- daily morning stretches for 15 minutes
- strength workout at the gym twice a week
- long walk/runs twice a week

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

- playing games with my family every Sunday - going out with friends in the evening at least twice a month - eating lunch together with the team at least every week

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

- journalling for 30 minutes every night before going to bed to brain dump, highlight a learning of the day or core memory, or expressing gratitude

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

- dedicate 15 minutes of my morning to reading and reflecting the Bible's verse of the day
- journalling for 30 minutes every night before going to bed to brain dump, highlight a learning of the day or core memory, or expressing gratitude

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

- long walk/runs twice a week
- hiking at least once a month
